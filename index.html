Introduction

Hello there!
I am Vivek, currently working as an ML Research Scientist at KLA . At KLA, I develop ML algorithms to support the metrology solution suite provided by KLA.

Prior to joining KLA, I was an Analytics & Insights Engineer at PARC (now SRI), developing structural health monitoring metrics through physics- and data-driven approaches. My work focused on defining sensing architectures to monitor the health of civil infrastructure using advanced sensors, big data analytics, and finite element modeling.

I completed my PhD and MS at Princeton University under the guidance of Prof. Branko Glišić. Before moving to the US, I finished my undergraduate degree in Civil Engineering with Honors at IIT Bombay and a minor degree in Management from SJMSOM.

Thanks for visiting!
